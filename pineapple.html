RECIPE:



Ingredients:


For the Sponge Cake:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • 3 large eggs
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • ½ cup milk
  • ¼ cup oil (vegetable or sunflower)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

    • For the Filling and Topping:

  • 1 ½ cups whipped cream
  • 1 cup chopped canned pineapple (or fresh pineapple)
  • 8-10 pineapple slices (for decoration)
  • ¼ cup chopped cherries (optional, for garnish)

    • Instructions:





    1. Make the Sponge Cake:


    Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and line a rectangular cake pan (9x13 inch).

    In a large bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, and baking soda.

    In a separate bowl, whisk eggs and sugar until pale and fluffy.

    Add oil, vanilla extract, and milk to the egg mixture and mix well.

    Gradually fold the dry ingredients into the wet mixture until you have a smooth batter.

    Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.

    Let the cake cool completely before cutting.


    2. Prepare the Pineapple Syrup:

    In a small saucepan, combine pineapple juice and sugar. Heat over medium heat until the sugar dissolves. Let it cool.


    3. Assemble the Pastry:

    Once the cake is cooled, slice it into equal-sized rectangular pieces.
    Generously brush each cake slice with the pineapple syrup to make it moist.
    Spread whipped cream on top of each slice, then add a layer of chopped pineapple.


    4. Decorate:

    Frost the top and sides of the pastries with more whipped cream.
    Place pineapple slices on top of each pastry and garnish with cherries for a classic look.
    Optionally, sprinkle some pineapple pieces or additional whipped cream on top for extra flair.


    5. Chill and Serve:

    Refrigerate the pineapple pastries for at least 1-2 hours before serving, allowing the flavors to blend.



    Enjoy your light and fluffy Pineapple Pastries with their refreshing tropical flavor!
