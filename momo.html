RECIPE:



Ingredients:


For the Dough:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • Water (as needed)
    • For the Filling:

  • 1 cup finely chopped cabbage
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped carrots
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped spring onions
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped bell peppers (optional)
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, minced
  • 1-2 green chilies, finely choppe
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1-2 tbsp oil

    • Instructuions:




    Making the Dough:

    In a bowl, mix all-purpose flour and salt.
    Gradually add water and knead into a smooth, soft dough.
    Cover with a damp cloth and let it rest for 20-30 minutes.


    2. Preparing the Filling:

    Heat oil in a pan. Add minced garlic, ginger, and green chilies. Saut√© for a minute.
    Add onions and cook until they turn translucent.
    Add cabbage, carrots, bell peppers, and spring onions. Stir-fry on high heat for 3-4 minutes.
    Add soy sauce, black pepper, and salt. Mix well and cook for another 2 minutes. Turn off the heat and let the filling cool.


    3. Assembling the Momos:


    Take small portions of the dough and roll them into thin circles (about 3-4 inches in diameter).
    Place a spoonful of the filling in the center of each circle.
    Fold the edges and pleat to seal the momo. You can shape them in a half-moon or any other desired shape.


    4. Steaming:

    Prepare a steamer by greasing it with oil or using parchment paper to avoid sticking.
    Place the momos in the steamer, making sure they are not touching each other.
    Steam the momos for 10-12 minutes or until the dough turns translucent and shiny.


    5. Serving:


    Serve hot with spicy momo chutney or dipping sauce.

    Enjoy your delicious homemade steamed momos!
