RECIPE:



Ingredients:


For the Sushi Rice:

  • 1 1/4 cup water
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt

    • For the Chicken Filling:

  • 200g boneless chicken breast (cooked or grilled)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp mayonnaise (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

    • For the Sushi Roll:

  • 4-5 sheets of nori (seaweed)
  • 1 cucumber (cut into thin strips)
  • 1 avocado (sliced)
  • 1 carrot (thinly sliced)
  • Soy sauce (for serving)
  • Wasabi and pickled ginger (optional for serving)

    • Instructions:


    Cook the Sushi Rice:

    Rinse the sushi rice in cold water until the water runs clear. Drain and add it to a pot with 1 1/4 cups of water. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and cook for 15 minutes.
    Once cooked, remove the rice from heat and let it sit for 10 minutes.
    In a small bowl, mix rice vinegar, sugar, and salt until dissolved. Pour this over the cooked rice and gently fold it in. Let the rice cool to room temperature.


    Prepare the Chicken:

    Grill or pan-cook the chicken breast until fully cooked. Let it cool, then shred or slice it into thin strips.
    In a bowl, mix the chicken with soy sauce, sesame oil, black pepper, and mayonnaise (if using) for extra creaminess.


    Assemble the Sushi Rolls:

    Place a sheet of nori (shiny side down) on a bamboo sushi mat or a clean kitchen towel.
    Wet your hands to prevent sticking, then spread a thin, even layer of sushi rice over the nori, leaving about 1 inch at the top for sealing.
    Place a few strips of chicken, cucumber, avocado, and carrot horizontally in the center of the rice.


    Roll the Sushi:

    Using the bamboo mat or towel, carefully roll the sushi from the bottom, pressing gently to keep it tight. Seal the edge by moistening the exposed nori with water.
    Slice the sushi roll into bite-sized pieces using a sharp knife dipped in water.


    Serve:

    Serve the chicken sushi with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger on the side.



    Enjoy your homemade chicken sushi!
