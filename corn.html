RECIPE:



Ingredients:


  • 2 cups sweet corn kernels (fresh or frozen)

  • 3 tablespoons cornflour (cornstarch)

  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour (maida)

  • 1 teaspoon peri peri seasoning (store-bought or homemade)

  • ½ teaspoon red chili powder

  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder (optional)

  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice

  • Salt to taste

  • Oil (for deep frying)

  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)


    • Instructions:



    Preparing the Corn:

    If using fresh corn:
    Boil the corn kernels in water for 3-4 minutes until slightly tender, then drain and pat dry.

    If using frozen corn:
    Thaw the corn by soaking it in warm water for 5 minutes, drain, and pat dry.

    Coating the Corn:
    In a mixing bowl, add the cornflour, all-purpose flour, red chili powder, garlic powder (if using), and salt.
    Add the corn kernels to the bowl and toss them well, ensuring each kernel is coated evenly with the flour mixture. Add a few drops of water if needed to help the flour stick to the corn.

    Frying the Corn:
    Heat oil in a deep pan or kadai over medium heat.
    Once the oil is hot, carefully add the coated corn in small batches. Fry the corn until golden and crispy, which should take about 4-5 minutes per batch.
    Use a slotted spoon to remove the fried corn and place it on a paper towel to drain excess oil.

    Tossing with Peri Peri Seasoning:
    While the corn is still hot, sprinkle the peri peri seasoning and lemon juice over the fried corn. Toss well to coat the corn evenly with the seasoning.

    Serving:
    Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve immediately while hot and crispy.
