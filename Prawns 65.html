RECIPE:



Ingredients:


For Marinating the Prawns:
  • 500g prawns (cleaned and deveined)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp rice flour
  • 1 tbsp cornflour
  • 1 egg (optional for extra crispiness)
  • Salt to taste

    • Instructions:


    Marinate the Prawns:

    In a bowl, combine prawns with ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, lemon juice, cornflour, rice flour, and salt. If using an egg, add it now for extra crispiness.
    Mix well and marinate for 30 minutes.


    Frying the Prawns:

    Heat oil in a deep pan for frying.
    Fry the marinated prawns in small batches until they turn golden brown and crispy. This should take about 3-4 minutes per batch.
    Remove and drain on paper towels.


    Tempering:

    In a separate pan, heat 1 tbsp oil.
    Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds, and let them splutter.
    Add slit green chilies, curry leaves, and ginger-garlic paste. Sauté for a minute until aromatic.
    Sprinkle red chili powder, coriander powder, and black pepper powder, and sauté briefly.
    Toss the fried prawns in this tempering to coat them with the spices.


    Garnish and Serve:

    Drizzle lemon juice over the prawns and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.


    Serve hot as an appetizer or side dish.


    Enjoy your spicy and crispy Prawns 65!
