RECIPE:



Ingredients:


  • 2 boneless chicken breasts (pounded thin)
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper (optional for extra heat)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cornflour
  • 1 tsp paprika (extra for flour mixture)
  • Oil for deep frying

    • Assembling the Burger:


  • 4 burger buns
  • Lettuce leaves
  • 4 slices of cheese (optional)
  • Mayonnaise
  • Pickles (optional)
  • 1 tomato (sliced)

    • Marinate the Chicken:

    In a bowl, mix buttermilk, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper.
    Add the chicken breasts to the marinade and let them sit for at least 1 hour (or overnight in the refrigerator).


    Prepare the Coating:

    In another bowl, mix the flour, cornflour, baking powder, paprika, and a pinch of salt.


    Coating and Frying the Chicken:

    Heat oil in a deep pan for frying.
    Remove the marinated chicken from the buttermilk and let any excess drip off.
    Coat the chicken in the flour mixture, pressing it well to ensure the coating sticks.
    Fry the chicken fillets in hot oil for 5-7 minutes on each side until golden brown and cooked through.
    Drain the fried chicken on paper towels.


    Assembling the Burger:

    Toast the burger buns slightly.
    Spread mayonnaise on both sides of the bun.
    Place a lettuce leaf on the bottom bun, followed by the fried chicken fillet.
    Add a slice of cheese (optional), tomato slices, and pickles if using.
    Cover with the top bun.


    Serve:

    Serve the chicken burger hot with fries and a beverage.



    Enjoy your crispy, juicy chicken burger!
