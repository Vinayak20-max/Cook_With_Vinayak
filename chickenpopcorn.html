RECIPE:



Ingredients:


For Marinating the Chicken:

  • 500g boneless chicken (cut into small bite-sized pieces)
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk (or plain yogurt)
  • 1 tsp paprika or red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

    • For Coating:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cornflour
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • Salt to taste

    • Instructions:


    Marinate the Chicken:

    In a bowl, combine buttermilk, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, black pepper, salt, and lemon juice.
    Add the chicken pieces and marinate for at least 1 hour (or overnight in the refrigerator for better flavor).


    Prepare the Coating:

    In another bowl, mix all-purpose flour, cornflour, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, black pepper, and salt.


    Coat the Chicken:

    Remove the chicken from the marinade and let any excess drip off.
    Dredge the chicken pieces in the flour mixture, ensuring each piece is evenly coated. Press the coating onto the chicken for a crispy texture.


    Fry the Chicken:

    Heat oil in a deep pan or fryer.
    Fry the coated chicken pieces in batches until they turn golden brown and crispy (about 4-5 minutes). Ensure they cook through but don't burn.
    Drain on paper towels.

    Optional: Toss in Seasoning

    After frying, you can toss the chicken popcorn with a mix of red chili powder, garlic powder, and chaat masala for an extra burst of flavor.


    Serve:

    Serve hot with ketchup, spicy mayo, or your favorite dipping sauce.



    Enjoy your crispy and delicious chicken popcorn!
