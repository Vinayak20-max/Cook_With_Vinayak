RECIPE:



Ingredients:


For the Chocolate Sponge Cake:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • ½ cup milk
  • ¼ cup oil (vegetable or sunflower)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

    • For the Chocolate Ganache:

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips (or dark chocolate, chopped)

    • For the Filling and Topping:

  • 1 ½ cups whipped cream
  • ½ cup chocolate shavings or curls (for decoration)

    • Instructions:




    1. Make the Chocolate Sponge Cake:

    Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and line a rectangular cake pan (9x13 inch).

    In a large bowl, sift together flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and baking soda.

    In a separate bowl, beat the eggs and sugar until light and fluffy.

    Add oil, vanilla extract, and milk to the egg mixture and whisk until combined.

    Gradually fold the dry ingredients into the wet mixture, stirring until smooth.

    Pour the batter into the prepared pan and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.

    Let the cake cool completely.


    2. Prepare the Chocolate Syrup:

    In a small saucepan, combine water, sugar, and cocoa powder.
    Heat the mixture over medium heat, stirring until the sugar dissolves.
    Let it cool.


    3. Make the Chocolate Ganache:


    Heat the cream in a small saucepan over medium heat until it starts to simmer (do not boil).
    Pour the hot cream over the chocolate chips in a heatproof bowl and let sit for 1-2 minutes.
    Stir until the chocolate is melted and the ganache is smooth. Let it cool to thicken slightly.


    4. Assemble the Pastry:

    Once the cake is completely cool, slice it into rectangular or square pieces.
    Brush each slice generously with the chocolate syrup to make it moist.
    Spread a layer of whipped cream between two cake slices to make a sandwich.


    5. Frost with Ganache:

    Pour the cooled, thickened chocolate ganache over the top of each pastry, letting it drip down the sides for a glossy finish.


    6. Decorate:

    Top each pastry with whipped cream swirls and sprinkle chocolate shavings or curls for an elegant touch.


    7. Chill and Serve:

    Refrigerate the pastries for at least 1-2 hours before serving, allowing the flavors to set.



    Enjoy your rich and indulgent Chocolate Pastries! Perfect for any special occasion or just to satisfy a chocolate craving.
