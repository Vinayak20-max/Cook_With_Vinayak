RECIPE:



Ingredients:


  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 tea bag (black or green tea)
  • 1 tablespoon of freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • Honey or sugar to taste (optional)
  • Lemon slices for garnish (optional)


    • Instructions:




    Boil Water:

    In a small pot or kettle, bring the water to a boil.


    Steep the Tea:

    Once the water is boiling, add the tea bag and steep for about 3-5 minutes, depending on how strong you like your tea.


    Add Lemon:

    Remove the tea bag and stir in the lemon juice.


    Sweeten (Optional):

    Add honey or sugar to taste, if desired.


    Serve:

    Pour into a cup and garnish with a lemon slice if you like.
