RECIPE:



Ingredients:



For chicken:

  • 500g chicken (cut into medium pieces)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice

    • For Rice:

  • 2 cups basmati rice (soaked for 30 minutes)
  • 4-5 cloves
  • 2-3 cardamom pods
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt to taste
    • ,
    For Biryani:

  • 2 large onions (thinly sliced and fried until golden brown)
  • 2 medium tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2-3 green chilies (slit)
  • 1 cup fresh coriander leaves (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup mint leaves (chopped)
  • 1 tsp biryani masala
  • 1/2 cup milk (with a pinch of saffron soaked)
  • 3 tbsp ghee or oil


    • Instructions:





    Marinate the Chicken:

    Mix the chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, salt, and lemon juice.
    Marinate for at least 1 hour (preferably overnight in the refrigerator).


    Prepare the Rice:

    Boil water in a large pot, add cloves, cardamom, cinnamon, bay leaf, and salt.
    Add soaked rice and cook until 70% done (al dente). Drain and set aside.


    Cooking the Chicken:

    Heat ghee or oil in a large pot, add the marinated chicken, and cook on medium heat until the chicken is tender and oil starts to separate.
    Add chopped tomatoes, green chilies, biryani masala, and cook for another 5-7 minutes.


    Layering:

    In the pot with the cooked chicken, layer half of the rice over the chicken.
    Sprinkle some fried onions, coriander, mint, and saffron milk.
    Add the remaining rice and top with more fried onions, herbs, and saffron milk.


    Dum Cooking:

    Cover the pot tightly (use a dough seal if needed) and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.


    Serve:

    Fluff the rice gently with a fork, and serve hot with raita or a side of your choice.



    Enjoy your flavorful Chicken Biryani!
