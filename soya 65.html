RECIPE:



Ingredients:


  • 1 cup soya chunks (soaked in hot water)
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 2 tbsp rice flour (optional for extra crispiness)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp red chili sauce (optional)
  • Few curry leaves (for garnish)
  • Oil (for deep frying)


    • Instructions:



    Prepare the Soya Chunks:

    Soak the soya chunks in hot water for 15-20 minutes until they soften.
    Drain and squeeze out the excess water from the soya chunks.


    Marinate the Soya:

    In a bowl, mix cornflour, all-purpose flour, rice flour, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, garam masala, black pepper, salt, soy sauce, and lemon juice.
    Add a little water to make a thick batter.
    Coat the soya chunks in this batter and let them marinate for 10-15 minutes.


    Fry the Soya:

    Heat oil in a deep frying pan or kadhai.
    Deep fry the marinated soya chunks until golden brown and crispy. Drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.


    Tempering and Garnishing:

    In a small pan, heat 1 tablespoon of oil.
    Fry a few curry leaves and green chilies for garnish.
    Add the fried curry leaves to the crispy soya chunks.


    Serve:

    Garnish with lemon wedges, extra fried green chilies, and sprinkle some chaat masala if desired.
    Serve hot with green chutney, ketchup, or as a stand-alone snack.


    Enjoy your crispy, flavorful Soya 65 as a great vegetarian snack or side dish!
