RECIPE:



Ingredients:


  • 2 cups grated mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup boiled and mashed potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons cornflour
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili flakes
  • 1/2 teaspoon mixed herbs (oregano, thyme, or Italian seasoning)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying
  • Bread crumbs (for coating)


    • Instructions:
    Prepare the mixture:

    In a large bowl, combine the grated mozzarella cheese, boiled and mashed potatoes. Add all-purpose flour, cornflour, black pepper powder, red chili flakes, mixed herbs, and salt. Mix well until everything is combined into a dough-like consistency.


    Shape the balls:

    Take small portions of the mixture and roll them into small balls using your palms. Coat each cheese ball with breadcrumbs for a crispy outer layer.


    Fry the cheese balls:

    Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.

    Gently drop the cheese balls into the hot oil and fry until they turn golden brown and crispy on all sides. This should take about 2-3 minutes per batch. Once done, remove and drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.

    Serve:


    Serve hot with ketchup, mayonnaise, or your favorite dip!


    Enjoy your delicious, gooey cheese balls!
