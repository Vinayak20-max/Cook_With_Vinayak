RECIPE:



Ingredients:


For marination:

  • 500g boneless chicken (cut into bite-sized cubes)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tbsp cornflour
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt and pepper to taste

    • For Frying:

  • 1/2 cup cornflour
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • Oil for deep frying

    • For Sauce:

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 onion (diced)
  • 1 green bell pepper (diced)
  • 1 red bell pepper (diced)
  • 4-5 green chilies (slit)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 2 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tbsp chili sauce
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp cornflour (mixed with 2 tbsp water to form a slurry)
  • Spring onions (for garnish)

    • Instructions:


    Marinate the Chicken:

    In a bowl, mix chicken cubes with soy sauce, vinegar, cornflour, ginger-garlic paste, salt, and pepper. Marinate for 30 minutes.


    Coat and Fry the Chicken:

    In a separate bowl, combine cornflour and all-purpose flour.
    Heat oil in a deep pan.
    Coat the marinated chicken cubes in the flour mixture and deep-fry until golden and crispy. Drain on paper towels.


    Prepare the Sauce:

    Heat 2 tbsp oil in a wok or large pan.
    Add diced onions and sauté until slightly translucent.
    Add diced green and red bell peppers, and slit green chilies. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes, keeping the vegetables slightly crunchy.
    Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté until aromatic.
    Stir in soy sauce, vinegar, ketchup, chili sauce, sugar, salt, and pepper. Cook for 2-3 minutes.


    Combine and Thicken:

    Add the fried chicken to the sauce and toss to coat.
    Add the cornflour slurry to the pan and cook until the sauce thickens slightly and coats the chicken evenly.


    Garnish and Serve:

    Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot with fried rice or noodles.



    Enjoy your flavorful and spicy chicken chilli!
