RECIPE:



Ingredients:


For the Chocolate Cake:

  • 1 and 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 and 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 and 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 and 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

    • For the Filling:

  • 1 ½ cups Nutella
  • ¾ cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup crushed hazelnuts
  • 8-10 Ferrero Rocher chocolates (chopped)

    • For the Frosting:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • ½ cup Nutella
  • 1 cup crushed hazelnuts
  • 6-8 Ferrero Rocher chocolates (whole, for decoration)

    • Instructions:




    1. Make the Cake:

    Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and line two 9-inch cake pans.
    In a large bowl, sift together flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and baking soda.
    In another bowl, beat sugar, eggs, oil, and vanilla extract until smooth.
    Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with milk, until the batter is smooth.
    Stir in the hot water until the batter is runny.
    Divide the batter evenly between the two pans and bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
    Let the cakes cool completely on a wire rack.


    2. Make the Filling:


    In a medium saucepan, heat the heavy cream until it begins to simmer. Remove from heat and stir in Nutella.
    Fold in the crushed hazelnuts and chopped Ferrero Rocher chocolates.
    Refrigerate the filling for about 15-20 minutes to thicken slightly.


    3. Make the Frosting:

    In a large bowl, whip the heavy cream until stiff peaks form.
    Gently fold in Nutella and crushed hazelnuts, mixing until fully combined.


    4. Assemble the Cake:

    Place the first layer of cake on a serving plate.
    Spread the Nutella and hazelnut filling evenly on top.
    Place the second cake layer over the filling.
    Frost the top and sides of the cake with the Nutella frosting.


    5. Decorate:

    Sprinkle extra crushed hazelnuts over the top and sides.
    Arrange Ferrero Rocher chocolates around the edge or center of the cake for an elegant finish.
    Refrigerate the cake for at least 1 hour before serving to allow the flavors to meld.



    Enjoy your decadent Ferrero Rocher cake!
