RECIPE:



Ingredients:


  • 6-8 Oreo cookies
  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup cold milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract (optional)
  • Whipped cream (for topping)
  • Crushed Oreos or chocolate syrup (for garnish)

    • Instructions:




    1. Blend the Milkshake:

    In a blender, add the vanilla ice cream, milk, Oreo cookies, and vanilla extract (if using).
    Blend until the mixture is smooth and creamy, with the cookies well incorporated.


    2. Serve:

    Pour the milkshake into tall glasses.


    3. Garnish:

    Top with whipped cream.
    Garnish with crushed Oreos or drizzle chocolate syrup over the whipped cream for an extra treat.


    4. Enjoy:

    Serve immediately with a straw for a delightful, creamy Oreo treat!



    This Oreo Milkshake is perfect for a refreshing dessert or a sweet snack!
