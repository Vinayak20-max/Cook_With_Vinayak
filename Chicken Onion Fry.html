RECIPE:



Ingredients:


  • 500g chicken (boneless, cut into small cubes)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp yogurt (curd)
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • 2 tbsp rice flour
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Oil for deep frying
  • Few curry leaves
  • 2-3 green chilies (slit)
  • 2 large onions (thinly sliced)


    • Instructions:




    Step 1: Prepare the Onion Barista

    Slice the onions thinly and uniformly.
    Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
    Add the sliced onions in batches and fry them until golden brown and crispy.
    Drain on paper towels to remove excess oil. These fried onions will be used for garnishing.


    Step 2: Marinate the Chicken

    In a bowl, add the chicken pieces and marinate with ginger-garlic paste, yogurt, egg, red chili powder, black pepper, turmeric, cumin powder, garam masala, salt, and lemon juice.
    Mix well and let it marinate for at least 30 minutes (the longer, the better).


    Step 3: Coat the Chicken

    Once the chicken is marinated, add cornflour and rice flour to the mixture. Coat the chicken pieces evenly.


    Step 4: Fry the Chicken

    Heat oil in a deep pan or kadai over medium heat.
    Deep fry the marinated chicken pieces in batches until they are golden and crispy on the outside. Drain on paper towels.


    Step 5: Final Tempering

    In a separate pan, heat a little oil. Add curry leaves and slit green chilies. Saut√© until fragrant.
    Toss the fried chicken pieces in this tempering to coat them with the aroma of the curry leaves and chilies.


    Step 6: Garnish and Serve

    Place the crispy Chicken 65 on a serving plate.
    Top with the crispy Onion Barista and some chaat masala.
