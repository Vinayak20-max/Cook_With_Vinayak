RECIPE:



Ingredients:


For the Chicken:

  • 250g boneless chicken (cut into small pieces)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp cornflour
  • Salt to taste

    • For the Rice:

  • 2 cups cooked basmati or jasmine rice (preferably a day old)
  • 2 eggs (beaten)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 carrot (finely chopped)
  • 1/2 cup peas (optional)
  • 1/2 cup spring onions (chopped)
  • 2-3 garlic cloves (finely chopped)
  • 2-3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp chili sauce (optional)
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tbsp oil (vegetable or sesame)
  • Salt to taste

    • Instructions:


    Marinate and Cook the Chicken:

    In a bowl, mix chicken with soy sauce, ginger-garlic paste, black pepper, cornflour, and a little salt. Let it marinate for 20-30 minutes.
    Heat 1 tbsp oil in a pan and stir-fry the marinated chicken until cooked through. Set aside.


    Scramble the Eggs:

    In the same pan, add a little oil and scramble the eggs until just cooked. Remove and set aside.


    Stir-Fry Vegetables:

    Add more oil if needed, then saut√© the chopped garlic and onion until softened.
    Add chopped carrots, peas, and any other vegetables you like. Stir-fry until slightly tender but still crisp.


    Combine Everything:

    Add the cooked chicken and scrambled eggs back into the pan with the vegetables.
    Add the cooked rice and toss everything together on high heat.
    Add soy sauce, vinegar, chili sauce (if using), black pepper, and salt to taste. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until everything is well-mixed and heated through.


    Garnish and Serve:

    Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot with extra soy sauce or chili sauce on the side.



    Enjoy your flavorful chicken fried rice!
